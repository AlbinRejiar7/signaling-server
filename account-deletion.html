Account Deletion - SyncFlix - Watch Party

Effective date: February 16, 2026

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open Profile.
  2. Go to Security.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm deletion.

For email/password accounts, password confirmation may be required before deletion can be completed.

What Is Removed

  • Authentication account credentials
  • Core profile record
  • Username index record
  • Stored profile image tied to the account (if present)

Some operational or diagnostic records may be retained where necessary for security, legal, or abuse-prevention purposes.

Need Help?

Use the in-app Report an Issue option from Profile -> Support.
